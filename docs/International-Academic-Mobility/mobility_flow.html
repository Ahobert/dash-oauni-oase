Mobility flows

within four country groups based on their income level

This figure displays the movements within four groups of countries categorized based on their income level at three career stages. We counted the movements among all authors. The left/right side is related to the origin/destination country. The highst mobility flow happen between high income level countries. at the later career stage we obverve increasing the number of movement toward countries with lower income levels. * H: High level, UM: Upper middle level, LM: Lower middle level, and L: Low level.
